Best Time to Visit Germany

Every season offers something different — find the perfect time for your trip.

When to Visit Germany

Germany is a year-round destination, but different seasons offer very different experiences. The best time for your visit depends on what you are hoping to do, whether that is hiking in the Alps, attending Oktoberfest, visiting Christmas markets, or exploring vibrant cities.

Season by Season

Spring (March – May)

Spring is a wonderful time to visit Germany. The weather is mild, the countryside blooms with colour, and the tourist crowds are smaller than in summer. April and May are particularly pleasant, with longer days and beautiful blossoms in the parks and gardens.

  • Temperatures: 8°C to 18°C (46°F to 64°F)
  • Best for: city breaks, cycling, Rhine Valley visits
  • Crowds: low to moderate — good value for accommodation

Summer (June – August)

Summer is Germany's most popular tourist season. The weather is warm and sunny, beer gardens are in full swing, and outdoor festivals take place across the country. The Black Forest, Bavarian Alps, and Baltic coast are all at their best.

  • Temperatures: 20°C to 30°C (68°F to 86°F)
  • Best for: outdoor activities, hiking, cycling, beach holidays
  • Crowds: high — book accommodation and transport in advance

Autumn (September – November)

Autumn is arguably the best season to visit Germany. The famous Oktoberfest takes place in late September and early October. The countryside turns beautiful shades of gold and red, and the weather is still pleasant in early autumn.

  • Temperatures: 10°C to 20°C (50°F to 68°F)
  • Best for: Oktoberfest, wine festivals, hiking, fewer crowds
  • Crowds: very high in Munich during Oktoberfest — moderate elsewhere

Winter (December – February)

Winter in Germany is magical — especially in December when Christmas markets appear in every town and city. Skiing in the Bavarian Alps is excellent, and the country has a cosy, festive atmosphere. January and February are the quietest months for tourism.

  • Temperatures: -2°C to 5°C (28°F to 41°F)
  • Best for: Christmas markets, skiing, winter sports, low prices
  • Crowds: high in December (Christmas season), very low in January–February

Our Recommendation

The ideal time to visit Germany is late May to June (pleasant spring weather, minimal crowds) or September (warm weather, Oktoberfest, autumn foliage beginning). For the best value and the most magical atmosphere, December for Christmas markets is unbeatable.
