Accommodation in Germany

Find the perfect place to stay — from budget hostels to luxury hotels.

Where to Stay in Germany

Germany offers a wide variety of accommodation options to suit every budget and travel style. Whether you are looking for a budget hostel, a comfortable mid-range hotel, a charming guesthouse, or a luxury five-star property, you will find excellent choices across the country.

Types of Accommodation

Hotels

Germany has hotels in every price range, from budget chains to luxury international brands. Most hotels include breakfast (Frühstück) — often an elaborate buffet with breads, cold meats, cheeses, and pastries.

Guesthouses (Pensionen)

Family-run guesthouses offer a personal, cosy alternative to hotels. Usually more affordable, with home-cooked breakfast and local knowledge from the owners.

Hostels (Jugendherbergen)

Germany has an excellent network of youth hostels (DJH) — clean, well-organised, and very affordable. Open to all ages. Located in scenic areas and city centres alike.

Holiday Apartments

Self-catering apartments (Ferienwohnungen) are popular and widely available, especially for longer stays. Platforms like Airbnb and local rental sites list many options.

Camping

Germany has hundreds of well-equipped campsites, particularly in scenic rural areas like Bavaria, the Black Forest, and the coast. Glamping options are also growing in popularity.

Castle Hotels

Germany has numerous historic castles and manor houses converted into hotels. Staying in a castle is a truly unique experience and many are surprisingly affordable.

Booking Tips

Book accommodation well in advance if visiting during Oktoberfest (September–October), Christmas market season (November–December), or major events. Prices in Munich during Oktoberfest can triple compared to regular periods.

  • hrs.com — German hotel booking platform with good corporate rates
  • jugendherberge.de — official German Youth Hostel Association
  • airbnb.com — apartments and private rooms across Germany
  • camping.info — database of campsites throughout Germany and Europe
